Introduction

Who is KOMBIT?

KOMBIT is the IT project organization of the Danish municipalities. Founded to create cohesion and efficiency, KOMBIT purchases, manages, and develops IT solutions on behalf of the 98 municipalities in Denmark.

By centralizing IT procurement and management, KOMBIT ensures that critical infrastructure—serving millions of citizens—is secure, compliant, and cost-effective.

Why ValueEdge (CSDP)?

To manage complex municipal IT projects, KOMBIT requires a robust testing foundation. Testing as a Service (TaaS) combined with OpenText ValueEdge delivers exactly that.

TaaS & CSDP Integration

Testing as a Service provides on-demand testing expertise and scalable resources. ValueEdge acts as the engine, providing a unified methodology across all of KOMBIT's testing initiatives.

Supplier Tech-Stack

KOMBIT works with external vendors. ValueEdge seamlessly integrates across diverse tech-stacks (Jira, Git, CI/CD tools), ensuring quality metrics are captured.

Combined Data

A single source of truth. By pulling data from automation and pipelines, ValueEdge gives leadership real-time visibility into readiness and risk.
