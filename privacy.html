Privacy Policy

We believe in full transparency about how your data is handled. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

Last Updated April 2026
Jurisdiction Denmark · EU (GDPR)
Version 1.0
01
Who We Are ⌄

This portal is operated by KOMBIT, Denmark. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in accordance with the GDPR.

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at qxmbg@kombit.dk.

02
What Data We Collect ⌄

When you create an account, we collect only the minimum necessary personal data:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Location data

We do not collect payment information, or behavioural tracking data.

03
Why We Collect It ⌄

Your data is collected solely to operate this portal. It is used to:

  • Identify you as a registered user
  • Allow you to sign in securely
  • Send account-related communications such as password resets
04
How We Use Your Data ⌄

Your personal data is not used for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any third-party purposes.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties under any circumstances.

05
Data Storage & Security ⌄

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a GDPR-compliant authentication and database platform.

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption protocols.

06
Your Rights (GDPR) ⌄

As an EU user, you have the following rights under GDPR:

Right of Access

Request a copy of your data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your account

Right to Restriction

Limit how we use your data

Right to Portability

Receive data in portable format

Right to Object

Object to data processing

To exercise any right, contact qxmbg@kombit.dk.

07
Cookies ⌄

This portal uses only cookies that are strictly necessary for authentication and session management.

We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

08
Data Retention ⌄

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active.

Upon account deletion request, all associated personal data will be permanently removed within 14 days.

09
Changes to This Policy ⌄

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Continued use of the portal constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Have a privacy question? We're committed to transparency. Reach out and we'll respond promptly.
